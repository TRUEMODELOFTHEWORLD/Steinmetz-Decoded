## Source Structure

The OCR candidate states the structural idea: the general solution includes a permanent term and a transient term.

In modern form:

$$
x(t) = x_{\text{permanent}}(t) + x_{\text{transient}}(t)
$$
For a simple first-order decay:

$$
x_{\text{transient}}(t) = A e^{-t/\tau}
$$

## Physical Meaning

The transient term exists because energy storage cannot change instantly. Inductance resists instantaneous current change. Capacity resists instantaneous voltage change. The circuit must move from one energy state to another.

<LayeredReading label="Steinmetz To Modern Translation" open>
Steinmetz's "permanent term" maps naturally to forced or steady-state response. His "transient term" maps to natural response. The exact mapping depends on the circuit and source.
</LayeredReading>
