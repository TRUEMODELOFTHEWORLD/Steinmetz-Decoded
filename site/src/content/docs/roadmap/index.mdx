## Goal

The immediate goal is a live, useful Steinmetz research archive, not a finished encyclopedia. A rigorous public resource can go live while clearly marking which layers are source-verified, candidate, interpretive, or still pending.

The full requirement map is tracked in the [Project Tracker](/project-tracker/) and in the repo-level `MASTER_PROJECT_TRACKER.md`.

The final expert finishing standard is tracked in [World-Class Completion Criteria](/roadmap/world-class-completion-criteria/), and the generated source-by-source readiness report is published at [Completion Audit](/roadmap/completion-audit/).

The scholarly operating layer now has public controls for [Citation And Data Export](/roadmap/citation-and-data-export/), [Editorial Policy](/roadmap/editorial-policy/), [Canonical Review Workflow](/roadmap/canonical-review-workflow/), [Canonical Verification Workbench](/roadmap/canonical-verification-workbench/), [Equation Verification Queue](/roadmap/equation-verification-queue/), [Figure Verification Queue](/roadmap/figure-verification-queue/), [Patent Verification Queue](/roadmap/patent-verification-queue/), [Claim Attribution Ledger](/roadmap/claim-attribution-ledger/), [Notation Ledger](/roadmap/notation-ledger/), [Diagram Provenance Ledger](/roadmap/diagram-provenance-ledger/), [Schema Reference](/roadmap/schema-reference/), [Expert Review Packets](/roadmap/expert-review-packets/), [Release Levels](/roadmap/release-levels/), [Accessibility Audit](/roadmap/accessibility-audit/), [Edition Comparison Layer](/roadmap/edition-comparison-layer/), and [Patent To Theory Bridge](/roadmap/patent-to-theory-bridge/).

The future multi-collection design is tracked separately in [Future Codex Architecture](/roadmap/future-codex-architecture/). That future scope is intentionally downstream of finishing the Steinmetz foundation.

## Live-Ready Core

The archive is live-ready when these conditions are met:

- The source library is visible and honest about processing state.
- The first book has original scan crops, source overview, lecture map, and at least one deep lecture page.
- The AC source has symbolic method, impedance, reactance, admittance, power-factor, and original Chapter V figure entry points.
- The transient source has original figure anchors for starting current, condenser charge, oscillation, and decrement.
- The transient source has permanent/transient term, oscillation, damping, surge, and distributed-line entry points.
- The glossary explains obsolete terms such as condensive reactance and wattless component.
- Interpretive pages are clearly separated from historical and mathematical claims.
- GitHub Pages deploys automatically from `main`.

## Rule For Speed

Move quickly by publishing labeled layers, not by lowering standards. Candidate pages are allowed. Unlabeled certainty is not.
