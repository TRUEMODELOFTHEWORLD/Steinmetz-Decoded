This ledger is the guardrail that lets the archive grow without mixing layers. ## Layer Rules

| Layer | Meaning | Rule |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `source_custody` | A source, edition, scan, OCR file, or custody pointer exists. | Use as a source pointer, not as proof that every page claim is verified. |
| `steinmetz_original_wording` | A candidate direct wording or close source passage from Steinmetz. | Requires scan verification before quotation as a settled text. |
| `mathematical_source_candidate` | A formula, equation, derivation, or numerical relation found in OCR/PDF text. | Check typography, symbols, signs, subscripts, and surrounding definitions before canonical use. |
| `modern_engineering_interpretation` | A modern translation of a Steinmetz expression or concept. | Keep visibly downstream from the source form it translates. |
| `concept_index_candidate` | A source-text occurrence or concept-routing signal. | Use to find passages, not as a final concept definition. |
| `glossary_candidate` | A historical term or obsolete-language signal requiring definition work. | Promote only after exact source usage and modern equivalent are checked. |
| `source_scan_asset` | A crop or image derived from an original source scan. | Review page number, figure label, crop completeness, and checksum before canonical diagram use. |
| `diagram_candidate` | A figure, diagram, waveform, or geometric representation candidate. | Connect to original scan asset where possible before explaining it. |
| `patent_authority_candidate` | A patent record with authority URL/PDF seed data. | Extract claims and drawings before using the patent as technical evidence. |
| `interpretive_reading` | Ether-field, Wheeler-style, Tesla-era, or other interpretive synthesis. | Must never be presented as what Steinmetz explicitly said unless independently sourced. |

## Source-Isolation Rule

When the archive later adds Tesla, Dollard, Russell, Wheeler, or any wider research collection, every record must keep its `person`, `collection`, `source_id`, `claim_type`, `interpretation_layer`, `confidence`, and `verification_required` fields. Comparison pages may synthesize across people only after each source layer is independently visible.
