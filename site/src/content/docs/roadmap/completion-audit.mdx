## Definitive-Archive Gate

The archive becomes definitive only when the readiness layer is followed by expert review:

- Priority sources have source custody, page maps, and checksums.
- Major equations are scan-verified, preserved in Steinmetz notation, and translated into modern notation.
- Major diagrams have original crops, modern redraws, and source-page references.
- Concept and glossary pages cite exact locations instead of relying on OCR occurrence counts.
- Tesla-era, ether-field, and other interpretive pages remain visibly separated from verified Steinmetz claims.
- Patents are authority-checked against official patent records and linked to drawings/PDFs where possible.
