## Seed Terms

The glossary is not just a dictionary. It is a translation layer between Steinmetz's historical electrical language and modern terminology. A mature term page should show source wording, modern equivalent, mathematical meaning, conceptual meaning, and whether the term is still used, renamed, narrowed, or obsolete.

## Glossary Method

Each term needs three layers before it becomes canonical:

1. Steinmetz usage with exact source location.
2. Modern engineering equivalent, including whether the term was renamed or narrowed.
3. Conceptual warning notes where modern shorthand hides the original physical picture.
