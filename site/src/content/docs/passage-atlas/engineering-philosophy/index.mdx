No legal enactment can change this, but the laws of nature are above man-made laws, and political law violating the laws of nature is void. You may destroy the indus- tries by legal interference, and plunge the na- tion in disaster and chaos, but you cannot re- store competition. It is dead, just as dead as the feudalism of the Middle Ages. Co-operation is taking its place.</p>
