CHAPTER XV. THE GENERAL ALTERNATING-CURRENT TRANSFORMER OR FREQUENCY CONVERTER. 141. The simplest alternating-current apparatus is the alternating-current transformer. It consists of a magnetic- circuit, interlinked with two electric circuits or sets of electric circuits. The one, the primary circuit, is excited by an impressed E.M.F., while in the other, the secondary circuit, an E.M.F. is induced.
