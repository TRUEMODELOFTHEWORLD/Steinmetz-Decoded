# Example Research Codex Charter

## Mission

Create a rigorous, readable, source-grounded public archive for `PRIMARY_SUBJECT`.

This is not a summary site. It is a research system that preserves source custody, original wording, mathematical or conceptual structure, historical context, modern interpretation, and clearly labeled speculative or alternative readings.

## Standard Layers

1. Source custody.
2. Cleaned text and page maps.
3. Concept extraction.
4. Equation or formal-structure extraction.
5. Diagram and visual extraction.
6. Glossary and terminology.
7. Hidden gems and important passages.
8. Modern explanation.
9. Historical context.
10. Labeled interpretive reading.
11. Verification queue.

## Quality Rule

No claim becomes canonical until it has a source anchor, review state, and explicit layer label.
